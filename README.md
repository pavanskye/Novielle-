# Novielle

AI-powered women's health companion delivering personalized, science-backed insights across every stage of life.

## What is Novielle?

Novielle is building the future of personalized women's health.

Our platform combines artificial intelligence, evidence-based health knowledge, and compassionate design to help women better understand their bodies throughout menstruation, fertility, pregnancy, postpartum recovery, and beyond.

Unlike traditional health trackers, Novielle focuses not only on collecting data, but on translating that data into meaningful, personalized insights.

## Core Capabilities

* Personalized cycle, pregnancy, and postpartum support
* AI-generated health insights and recommendations
* Daily physical and emotional wellness tracking
* PCOS-focused guidance and symptom monitoring
* Stress, recovery, and wellbeing intelligence
* Multilingual support for diverse populations
* Expert consultation and care pathways

## Our Mission

To ensure that no woman feels confused, unheard, or unsupported when it comes to her health.

## Vision

To become the world's most trusted AI companion for women's health.
